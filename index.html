Problem → Solution

Three urgent gaps in finance across Uzbekistan

We translate real banking pain points into deployable AI products.

A. Banking — Credit Risk

Low-accuracy scoring hurts banks and borrowers

  • Low accuracy of current credit scoring systems
  • High default rates and financial losses
  • Heavy manual work and human error
  • Slow processing of credit applications
  • Limited access to affordable lending for the population
Paired solution: AI Risk Scoring & Fraud Detection Platform
B. Financial Fraud

Fraud hides in fast-growing transaction flows

  • Growing transaction volume
  • Difficult to detect abnormal or suspicious operations
  • Banks lose significant money due to fraud
  • Manual monitoring is inefficient
Paired solution: AI Fraud model with explainable alerts
C. Lack of Investment Tools

Citizens have no safe way to learn investing

  • No accessible stock market for citizens
  • Low financial literacy
  • Young people cannot safely learn investing
  • Many fall victim to scammers and pseudo-investments
  • There is no sandbox environment for practicing investment skills
Paired solution: Investment Training Simulator

Solution

How we plan to implement the solution

Fast-moving, production-focused approach from data to deployment.

Data & Features

Unify banking data into clean datasets, engineer risk signals, and add fraud-specific behavioral features.

  • Data quality improvement and lineage tracking
  • Feature stores for credit and fraud signals
  • Real-time ingestion for streaming fraud checks

Models & Explainability

Train Scikit-Learn, CatBoost, and XGBoost models with AutoML baselines and XAI for decisions.

  • Credit PD models with calibrated thresholds
  • Fraud detectors with anomaly and rules hybrid
  • SHAP/LIME views embedded into dashboards

Products & Ops

Expose results via FastAPI, Streamlit/React dashboards, and CI/CD for rapid rollout.

  • Unified client risk profile with alerts
  • Operator-friendly credit scoring web app
  • Cloud deployment with Docker and GitHub Actions
